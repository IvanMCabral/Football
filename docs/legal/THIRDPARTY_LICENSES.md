## 1. Wikipedia (team name verification source)

**License:** Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International (CC-BY-SA 4.0)
**URL:** <https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/>
**Legal text:** <https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/legalcode>

### Usage

Team names in the C55.1 seed files were verified against Wikipedia articles for the 2024-25 football season (10 leagues). The articles are listed in `docs/legal/synthetic-data.md` section 2.1.

Wikipedia is used as a **verification source** for factual data (real-world football club names, country of origin, league membership). Wikipedia's textual content is NOT copied — only the names of real-world entities (which are factual data, not copyrightable creative expression) are extracted.

### Attribution

Per CC-BY-SA 4.0 § 3(b), attribution is provided by linking to the source articles (see `synthetic-data.md` section 2.1) and noting that Wikipedia is the verification source.

Per CC-BY-SA 4.0 § 3(c), derivative works (if any) must be shared under the same license. The C55.1 seed is **not a derivative work** of Wikipedia — it contains factual data only — so this clause does not apply. However, the spirit of CC-BY-SA is honored by attribution.

### Wikipedia community credit

Team name verifications relied on the work of **Wikipedia contributors**, who curate and maintain the articles linked in `synthetic-data.md` section 2.1. Their work is gratefully acknowledged.

## 2. Algorithmic stats (no third-party license required)

The player attributes in the seed are generated algorithmically by `PlayerAttributesGenerator` (original Java code under the project's license). No third-party data source contributes to the attribute values.

**License:** N/A (original creative work generated by the project's own algorithm)
**Algorithm:** see `src/main/java/com/footballmanager/application/service/world/PlayerAttributesGenerator.java`
